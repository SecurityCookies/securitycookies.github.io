About SecurityCookies

Once upon a time, Person1 and Person2 were joking about fortune cookies and how they could be used to make security awareness fun.

Then, after a few exchanges, it became clear that a batch had to be made for the 2014 editions of Blackhat, BsidesLV and DEF CON.

SecurityCookies with 15 different pieces of advice or fail-advice have been brought to Vegas. The cookies will be distributed by the creators and friends during various events.
